### ADC is supported in following cores:

* Acorn Electron
* ADCTest
* AliceMC10
* Amstrad
* Apple-II
* Commodore 64
* CoCo2
* Lynx 48 and 96
* Ondra SPO 186
* Oric
* SVI-328
* Tesla PMD 85-1
* ZX-Spectrum

ADCTest will help visualize and set volume levels and verify tape quality (and operation of the ADC itself).
